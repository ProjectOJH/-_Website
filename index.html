錢滾錢
MONEY BEGETS MONEY

錢滾錢象徵財富不斷增長與倍增。
擺放於家中或工作空間，寓意財運流動不息——資金持續運轉，機會接續而來。它代表穩健成長，以及隨時間累積而成的長遠富足。

錢滾錢 represents wealth that grows and multiplies. Placed in a home or workspace, it brings financial momentum — money constantly flowing, one opportunity leading to another. It reflects steady growth and sustainable building of abundance over time.

錢滾錢 框
FRAMED EDITION

承載著與「錢滾錢」相同的意涵，這款框架版象徵財富的流動——讓財運隨時間持續累積與增長。透過傳統華人能量轉化的原理（借遁），將「錢滾錢」的能量置入框架之中，使其自然融入家中或工作空間。它象徵財務動能——資金循環流轉，機會接續而至。

Carrying the same intention as 錢滾錢, this framed edition represents wealth in motion — prosperity that flows and builds over time. Through the traditional Chinese principle of energetic transference (借遁 · jie dun), the essence of 錢滾錢 is placed into a frame, allowing it to integrate seamlessly into your home or workspace. It symbolizes financial momentum — money in circulation and opportunities arriving with continuity.

占卜

Ancient Wisdom, Modern Clarity
塔囉聊心
XIAN TIAN TAROT

全世界塔羅牌有 888 種。

先天塔羅是唯一可以連結靈魂母元星宿的塔羅牌。

不一樣的人問同一個問題、抽到同一張牌、訊息完全不一樣，因爲先天塔羅牌不是看圖說故事，也不是藉由牌意來解牌。

先天塔羅每一張牌對應著天上的一個星宿，而先天塔羅師是藉由聖筆讀取與接收訊息——訊息來自妳／你的靈魂。

想跟靈魂聊聊？想釐清心之所嚮？想知道生由何來死又何去？想了解前世今生？

來試試看先天塔羅牌吧！

Across the world there are many forms of tarot, each with its own symbols and systems. Xian Tian Tarot takes a different path. It does not rely on memorized meanings or fixed interpretations. It does not attempt to predict or prescribe. Instead, it begins with the simple belief that clarity already lives within you.

The cards are not answers. They are mirrors. Each card serves as an open symbol — a doorway through which perception, memory, and intuition can surface. Because of this, no two readings are ever the same.

The name Xian Tian (先天) refers to what is innate — the underlying structure that exists before conscious thought. In this spirit, the practice looks not outward for answers, but inward toward what has always been present.

易經解困
I CHING READING

易經為萬經之首，自古帝王將相都要略懂一二，更甚至是古時候的軍師必修之法門。

所謂道統治國、易經治企業，而易經為道統之子、道統為易經之母，故易經為國醫。

真正的先天易經直指核心，屬真正命相，看其生有何來死又何去、算其命數星宿，更能針對問題、解決問題——從個人立、家庭和、事業興、社會平到國家盛，天困智慧、地困財富、人困健康。

先天易經有五大法門：易經卜卦天下知、奇門遁卦天下解、混沌沌卦天下藏、燃燈燈卦天下富、燈燈燈卦天下貴

The I Ching (Book of Changes) is one of the oldest foundational texts in Chinese thought and philosophy. For thousands of years, it has influenced governance, leadership, medicine, military strategy, and personal decision-making.

Today, it can be approached as a system for reflection, clarity, and strategic guidance in modern life — addressing challenges across wisdom, wealth, and health through five traditional methodologies.

療癒

Restore Your Body, Mind & Spirit
刀式舒壓
KNIFE THERAPY

刀療，源起於春秋戰國時代，之後傳到日本，目前正在台灣大力推廣並採用科學方法和理念，更有效地為需要的朋友服務。

人體本身有自療的本能，只是現代人的生活、飲食不當、心理因素或工作壓力，使得免疫系統失調、體內循環癱塞。刀療即是用放電原理，將電子（磁、氣、能量）擊入患部達到深層刺激，可深達肌裡直透骨髓，屬「非侵入性」之舒緩方法。

透過刀療，可以讓人感受到 SPA 的感覺——身體放鬆、促進新陳代謝、調整體質、減少疲勞、睡眠品質改善。

Deep Relaxation & Body Regulation Treatment

Knife Therapy is a traditional East Asian bodywork practice. Today, it is practiced using modern wellness principles and a gentle, non-invasive approach to support relaxation, circulation, and overall physical balance.

Modern stress, irregular sleep, and emotional strain can contribute to muscle tightness, poor circulation, fatigue, and reduced recovery. Knife Therapy uses specially designed, blunt tools applied rhythmically along the body's surface to provide deep, targeted stimulation without penetrating the skin.

Most people describe the session as deeply relaxing and grounding — clients often report lighter body sensation, reduced fatigue, improved sleep, clearer focus, and better overall wellbeing.

芬多精療癒
PHYTONCIDE HEALING

在日本的自然療法，又稱為森林浴，藉由深呼吸在美景中漫步達到療癒的效果。

芬多精是樹木釋放到空氣中的天然揮發性有機化合物。藉由珊瑚原礦的轉換，會調頻人體最舒適的波長，讓我們藉由芬多精療癒石來舒壓與放鬆。

研究表明芬多精可以：減少壓力（顯著降低心率和血壓）、增強免疫系統（提升自然殺手細胞活性）、改善心情（增加幸福感）、增進集中力和記憶力。

Nature-Inspired Stress Relief & Forest Bathing Experience

Inspired by the Japanese wellness concept of Shinrin-yoku (forest bathing) — the simple yet powerful act of slowing down, breathing deeply, and immersing yourself in nature.

Phytoncides are natural aromatic compounds released by trees and plants. Research suggests that time in forest environments may support stress reduction, relaxation of the nervous system, improved mood, enhanced focus and mental clarity, and general immune support.
